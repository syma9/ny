Clustering analysis: DBSCAN
Noise Complaints

 

Manhattan has most clusters of noise complaints. Washington Heights and Hamilton Heights were two hotspots in Upper Manhattan, while the hotspots were located in Central Harlem House and Yorkville in Upper East Side in 2016, and Grand Concourse in Bronx and Downtown Brooklyn in 2015. These are all highly-populated areas and the complaints might be due to the presence of noise from activities, such as the night life scene in East Village.
